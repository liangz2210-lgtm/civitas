## 摘要 Abstract

Civitas Protocol（CIV）是一个运行于公链之上的自适应基础收入协议（Adaptive Universal Basic Income Protocol）。

CIV 提出了一种新的货币范式：
- **比特币**锚定固定供应；
- **稳定币**锚定法币价格；
- **CIV** 锚定人类最低生存能力。

基于人格证明（Proof of Personhood）的全球 UBI；
基于市场价格的自适应发行机制；
基于交易费与财富衰减的循环经济引擎；
基于贡献度而非单纯资本的治理模型；
基于链上宪法层（Constitution Layer）的不可剥夺生存保障。

协议最终目标不是创造另一种投机资产，而是构建：
一个将最低生存保障、知识获取能力与全球协作能力嵌入互联网底层的文明基础协议。

## Part I — 哲学与价值观基础 / Philosophical Foundation

### 1. 核心问题：贫困是文明的效率损耗 / Poverty as Civilizational Efficiency Loss

现代文明拥有历史上最强大的生产能力，但仍有数十亿人缺乏稳定生存条件。

CIV 的核心观点是：**贫困不仅是道德问题，更是文明整体算力的系统性浪费。**

Modern civilization possesses the greatest productive capacity in history, yet billions still lack stable survival conditions.

CIV's core thesis: **Poverty is not merely a moral issue — it is a systemic waste of civilization's total computational power.**

#### 1.1 认知税理论 / Scarcity: The Cognitive Tax

资源匮乏会长期占用人的认知带宽。长期贫困状态会迫使个体不断将注意力投入食物、房租、医疗、生存风险，这会压制创造力、长期规划能力、学习能力、风险承担能力。

Resource scarcity chronically occupies cognitive bandwidth. Prolonged poverty forces individuals to continually direct attention toward food, rent, healthcare, and survival risk — suppressing creativity, long-term planning, learning ability, and risk-taking capacity.

因此：**基础收入不是对懒惰的奖励，而是对文明潜在生产力的释放。**

Therefore: **Basic income is not a reward for laziness — it is the liberation of civilization's latent productive power.**

### 2. 文明自由的物质基础 / Material Basis for Civil Liberty

#### 2.1 Rawls：无知之幕 / The Veil of Ignorance

如果一个理性的人无法提前知道自己出生于富裕还是贫穷、位于哪个国家、拥有什么能力，那么他更可能选择一个无论出生条件如何都能保障最低生存权的社会。CIV 将这一原则算法化。

If a rational person could not know in advance whether they would be born rich or poor, in which country, or with what abilities, they would more likely choose a society that guarantees minimum survival rights regardless of birth condition. CIV algorithmatizes this principle.

#### 2.2 Sen：能力方法 / The Capabilities Approach

CIV 保障的不是"钱"本身，而是：参与经济活动的能力、获取知识的能力、进行真实选择的能力、参与文明创造的能力。

CIV does not guarantee "money" per se, but rather: the capability to participate in economic activity, to acquire knowledge, to make genuine choices, and to participate in civilizational creation.

### 3. 货币的本质：流动而非囤积 / The Nature of Money: Circulation over Hoarding

#### 3.1 Gesell：自由货币 / Freigeld

货币的价值来自流通，而非长期冻结。交易应被鼓励，长期囤积应承担成本，财富循环优于财富沉积。CIV 的财富衰减（Wealth Decay）机制即来源于此。

The value of money comes from circulation, not long-term freezing. Transactions should be encouraged; long-term hoarding should bear costs; wealth circulation is superior to wealth deposition. CIV's Wealth Decay mechanism derives from this principle.

### 4. AI 与知识平权 / AI and Knowledge Equity

大语言模型（LLM）是历史上第一次：专业知识能够以接近零成本扩散到全球。知识不应再被少数机构垄断，AI 应成为知识平权工具，AI 应辅助治理而非统治治理。

LLMs represent the first time in history that specialized knowledge can diffuse globally at near-zero cost. Knowledge should no longer be monopolized by a few institutions; AI should serve as a knowledge equity tool; AI should assist governance, not dominate it.

## Part II — 宪法层 / Constitution Layer

### 5. 不可治理的协议底线 / Ungovernable Protocol Baseline

CIV 并非"任何事情都可投票修改"的系统。协议存在不可被治理层修改的**宪法不变量（Constitutional Invariants）**。

CIV is not a system where "anything can be modified by vote." The protocol possesses **Constitutional Invariants** that the governance layer cannot alter.

#### 5.1 生存权不可剥夺 / Inalienable Right to Survival

每一个经过验证的人格（Verified Personhood）必须拥有最低基础生存保障。

Every Verified Personhood must possess a minimum basic survival guarantee:
- 不得因投票取消 / Cannot be cancelled by vote
- 不得因财富差异取消 / Cannot be cancelled by wealth disparity
- 不得因地区差异取消 / Cannot be cancelled by regional disparity

#### 5.2 人格平等原则 / Personhood Equality Principle

每个验证人格：拥有平等基础 UBI 权；不因财富获得额外人格权；不因资本拥有更多基础生存资格。

Every verified personhood: holds equal basic UBI rights; gains no extra personhood rights from wealth; possesses no greater basic survival qualification from capital.

#### 5.3 自由退出原则 / Free Exit Principle

协议必须始终允许自由交易、自由持有、自由退出、自由兑换。协议不得强制消费、强制锁仓、强制资产绑定。

The protocol must always permit: free transaction, free holding, free exit, free exchange. The protocol must not: force consumption, force lock-up, force asset binding.

#### 5.4 社会扩展原则 / Social Extension Principle

未来随着生产力提升，协议可通过极严格治理程序扩展：基础医疗、基础教育、灾难保障、公共 AI 服务。但新增社会服务不得削弱基础 UBI。

As productivity rises, the protocol may extend via extremely strict governance procedures to: basic healthcare, basic education, disaster relief, public AI services. New social services must not diminish basic UBI.

## Part III — 自适应货币系统 / Adaptive Monetary System

### 6. 固定购买力模型 / Fixed Purchasing Power Model

CIV 的核心创新不是固定供应，而是**固定最低生存购买力**。

| 系统 System | 锚定对象 Anchor |
|---|---|
| Bitcoin | 固定供应 Fixed supply |
| Stablecoin | 固定美元价格 Fixed USD price |
| 法币福利 Fiat welfare | 固定法币金额 Fixed fiat amount |
| **CIV** | **固定最低生存购买力 Fixed minimum survival purchasing power** |

#### 6.1 自适应 UBI 发行公式 / Adaptive UBI Issuance Formula

$$UBI_{daily} = \frac{FCI}{EMA(P_{CIV})}$$

其中：FCI = Food Cost Index（食物成本指数），EMA(P_CIV) = CIV 市场价格指数的指数移动平均。

行为表现 / Behavioral implications:

| CIV价格 CIV Price | 每日发放量 Daily Issuance |
|---|---|
| $1 | 4 CIV |
| $100 | 0.04 CIV |
| $10,000 | 0.0004 CIV |

该机制允许代币自由升值、投机者自由参与，UBI 接收者始终获得接近固定的购买力。投资属性与生存保障属性被统一进同一种货币。

This mechanism allows: tokens to freely appreciate, speculators to freely participate, UBI recipients to always receive approximately fixed purchasing power. Investment and survival-guarantee attributes are unified in a single currency.

#### 6.2 核心意义 / Core Significance

投资属性与生存保障属性被统一进同一种货币。

Investment and survival-guarantee attributes are unified in a single currency.

#### 6.3 发行熔断机制 / Issuance Circuit Breaker

为防止价格崩盘导致的死亡螺旋，协议引入发行熔断。同时使用 EMA 平滑以防止短期价格操纵。

To prevent a death spiral from price collapse, the protocol introduces an issuance circuit breaker. EMA smoothing prevents short-term price manipulation.

#### 6.4 Oracle 系统 / Oracle System

协议采用：多交易所价格聚合、加权中位数、异常值剔除、EMA 平滑、速率限制。

The protocol employs: multi-exchange price aggregation, weighted median, outlier removal, EMA smoothing, rate limiting.

目标：防止单一来源操纵，防止闪崩诱导增发，保持长期稳定。

Goals: prevent single-source manipulation, prevent flash-crash-induced over-issuance, maintain long-term stability.

## Part IV — 身份层 / Identity Layer

### 7. 人格证明 / Proof of Personhood

UBI 的核心问题是女巫攻击（Sybil Attack）。协议目标：保证一个真实人类只能领取一份基础 UBI。

The core challenge of UBI is the Sybil Attack. Protocol goal: ensure each real human can claim only one basic UBI.

#### 7.1 分阶段身份方案 / Phased Identity Scheme

**Phase 1 — 社交图谱验证 / Social Graph Verification**

参考 BrightID、Proof of Humanity。特点：无需中心化身份、不上传生物信息、社会信任关系验证。

References: BrightID, Proof of Humanity. Features: no centralized identity, no biometric upload, social trust verification.

**Phase 2 — ZK 生物证明 / ZK Biometric Proof**

用户可选择提交零知识生物哈希，不暴露原始生物数据，获得更高可信度。

Users may optionally submit zero-knowledge biometric hashes without revealing raw biometric data, gaining higher confidence.

#### 7.2 反女巫机制 / Anti-Sybil Mechanisms

- 行为聚类检测 / Behavioral clustering detection
- 时间加权余额 / Time-weighted balances
- 独立对手方分析 / Independent counterparty analysis
- 中位数抗操纵 / Median manipulation resistance
- 推荐网络分析 / Referral network analysis

## Part V — 循环经济引擎 / Circular Economy Engine

### 8. 费用循环系统 / Fee Cycling System

CIV 的长期目标不是依赖无限增发。最终目标：UBI 主要来自经济循环本身。

CIV's long-term goal is not reliance on infinite issuance. Ultimate goal: UBI primarily comes from the economic cycle itself.

#### 8.1 交易手续费 / Transaction Fees

所有费用进入统一 Fee Pool。

All fees enter a unified Fee Pool.

#### 8.2 财富衰减 / Wealth Decay

$$decay = rate \times excess \times \frac{b}{m}$$

其中 b = 时间平均余额，m = 全网加权中位数。**低于中位数用户免征。**

Where b = time-averaged balance, m = network-wide weighted median. **Users below the median are exempt.**

目标：抑制长期沉积，提高货币流动性，减缓财富极化。

Goals: suppress long-term deposition, increase monetary velocity, slow wealth polarization.

#### 8.3 不活跃衰减 / Inactivity Decay

长期不参与经济循环的账户会逐步承担成本。

Accounts that long remain inactive in the economic cycle will gradually bear costs.

## Part VI — 激励层 / Incentive Layer

### 9. 行为即贡献 / Behavior as Contribution

协议目标不是鼓励纯持币，而是鼓励：交易、推广、协作、公共品建设。

The protocol aims not to encourage pure holding, but: transactions, promotion, collaboration, public goods construction.

#### 9.1 Activity Score

$$Score = TxScore + SocialScore$$

其中 TxScore = 交易贡献，SocialScore = 社交与知识贡献。

Where TxScore = transaction contribution, SocialScore = social and knowledge contribution.

#### 9.2 推荐机制 / Referral Mechanism

推荐奖励与长期活跃度绑定。不是一次性奖励。目标：抑制垃圾邀请，鼓励真实网络增长。

Referral rewards are bound to long-term activity — not one-time bonuses. Goals: suppress spam invitations, encourage genuine network growth.

## Part VII — 治理层 / Governance Layer

### 10. 治理层延迟启动 / Delayed Governance Activation

治理不会在协议早期立即开放。原因：用户分布不均、容易被捕获、认知尚未形成。

Governance does not open immediately in the protocol's early stages. Reasons: uneven user distribution, vulnerability to capture, cognition not yet formed.

| 阶段 Phase | 治理模式 Governance Mode |
|---|---|
| 0 → 1000万用户 users | 技术委员会 Technical Committee |
| 1000万 → 1亿 10M–100M | 半开放治理 Semi-open governance |
| 1亿+ 100M+ | 全民治理 Full democratic governance |

#### 10.1 四维治理权重 / Four-Dimensional Governance Weight

CIV 不采用纯资本投票。治理权重由四部分组成：

CIV does not use pure capital voting. Governance weight is composed of four parts:

$$W_u = f(B_u, T_u, R_u, D_u)$$

其中：B_u = 持币贡献，T_u = 流通贡献，R_u = 推广贡献，D_u = 捐赠贡献。

Where: B_u = holding contribution, T_u = circulation contribution, R_u = referral contribution, D_u = donation contribution.

目标：让治理权更接近真实网络贡献。

Goal: align governance power closer to genuine network contribution.

#### 10.2 分级治理程序 / Tiered Governance Procedures

**Level 1 — 参数调整 / Parameter Adjustment**

例如：交易费率、EMA 参数。要求：50% 多数 + 7 天 timelock。

E.g.: transaction fee rates, EMA parameters. Requires: 50% majority + 7-day timelock.

**Level 2 — 功能升级 / Feature Upgrade**

例如：接入新 Oracle、新身份系统。要求：66% 超多数 + 30 天 timelock。

E.g.: new Oracle integration, new identity system. Requires: 66% supermajority + 30-day timelock.

**Level 3 — 核心机制修改 / Core Mechanism Modification**

例如：修改 UBI 发行公式、修改发行上限。要求：80% 超超多数 + 180 天 timelock + 长期公开讨论。

E.g.: modify UBI issuance formula, modify issuance cap. Requires: 80% super-supermajority + 180-day timelock + extended public discussion.

**Level 4 — 宪法层 / Constitution Layer**

**不可治理 / Ungovernable.** 包括：最低生存权、人格平等、自由退出原则。

Includes: minimum survival right, personhood equality, free exit principle.

## Part VIII — 公共项目融资 / Public Project Financing

### 11. 三方公共品融资 / Trilateral Public Goods Financing

公共项目资金来源 / Public project funding sources:

$$Funding = SelfFunding + CommunityFunding + ProtocolMatching$$

#### 11.1 三方结构的意义 / Significance of the Trilateral Structure

**提案人自筹 / Self-Funding**: 确保提案人承担风险，防止垃圾提案。
Ensures proposers bear risk; prevents spam proposals.

**社区众筹 / Community Crowdfunding**: 作为真实需求信号。如果社区无人支持，则协议无需匹配。
Serves as genuine demand signal. If the community does not support it, the protocol need not match.

**协议匹配 / Protocol Matching**: 协议资金池用于放大公共品、支持长期价值创造、建设生态基础设施。
Protocol treasury used to amplify public goods, support long-term value creation, build ecosystem infrastructure.

## 结语 / Conclusion

CIV 不是一个"发钱项目"。它试图解决的问题是：如何让最低生存能力成为数字文明的默认配置。

农业革命解决了食物生产；工业革命解决了规模生产；数字革命解决了信息复制；AI 革命正在解决知识扩散。

CIV 的目标是：将最低生存保障本身，嵌入互联网底层。

协议不要求所有人认同。它只要求足够多的人相信：当每个人都拥有最低生存能力与知识获取能力时，人类文明整体将比今天更强大。

CIV is not a "money-giving project." The problem it seeks to solve: how to make minimum survival capacity the default configuration of digital civilization.

The agricultural revolution solved food production; the industrial revolution solved scale production; the digital revolution solved information replication; the AI revolution is solving knowledge diffusion.

CIV's goal: to embed minimum survival guarantee itself into the internet's foundation.

The protocol does not require everyone's agreement. It only requires enough people to believe: when every person possesses minimum survival capacity and knowledge acquisition ability, human civilization as a whole will be stronger than it is today.
